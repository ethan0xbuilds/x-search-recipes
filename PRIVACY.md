# Privacy Policy

**X Search Recipes** does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

## What the extension does

- Runs only on `x.com` and `twitter.com`.
- Reads the current page URL (to prefill the keyword from search pages).
- Stores your preferences and custom recipes in Chrome’s `chrome.storage.sync` (synced by your browser account if enabled).
- Builds search query strings and navigates to X search URLs in your browser.

## What the extension does not do

- No analytics or telemetry.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No remote servers or third-party APIs.
- No reading of direct messages, passwords, or unrelated page content beyond injecting the UI panel.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
