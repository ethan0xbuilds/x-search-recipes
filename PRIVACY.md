# Privacy Policy — X Search Recipes

**Last updated:** 2026-07-20

**X Search Recipes** (“the Extension”) respects your privacy. This policy describes what the Extension does and does not do with data.

## Summary

The Extension **does not** collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer or any third party.  
All preferences stay in your browser (Chrome storage).

## Data the Extension uses

| Data | Where it lives | Why |
|------|----------------|-----|
| Keyword you type in the panel | Memory only (not sent to us) | Build search queries |
| Threshold, sort, open-in-new-tab, panel position, custom recipes | `chrome.storage.sync` on your device / Chrome profile | Remember settings |
| Current page URL on x.com / twitter.com | Read locally in the content script | Prefill keyword from search pages |

## Data the Extension does **not** use

- No analytics or telemetry  
- No advertising IDs  
- No accounts or login for the Extension itself  
- No remote servers operated by us  
- No access to your X DMs, passwords, or payment info  
- No sale of data to third parties  

## Permissions

| Permission | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| `storage` | Save settings and custom recipes |
| Access to `x.com` and `twitter.com` | Show the panel and open search results on those sites |

## Third parties

The Extension only navigates your browser to X search URLs you trigger.  
X’s own privacy policy applies to your use of X: https://x.com/privacy  

We do not send Extension data to X beyond the normal navigation you initiate.

## Children

The Extension is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy in the repository. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.
